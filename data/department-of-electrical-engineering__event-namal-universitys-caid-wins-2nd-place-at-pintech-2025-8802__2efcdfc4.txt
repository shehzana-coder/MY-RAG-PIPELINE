Home Event Namal University's CAID Wins 2nd Place at PINTECH 2025 Event Latest From Namal Namal University's CAID Wins 2nd Place at PINTECH 2025 We’re thrilled to announce a proud moment for the Center of AI and Big Data (CAID) at Namal University! Our project “Virtual Reality-Based Tele-Rehabilitation System” secured 2nd place at the Punjab Innovation and Technology Expo 2025 (PINTECH 2025), held on April 21st at Expo Center, Lahore. Competing with top universities across Punjab, this cutting-edge solution showcased how AI and immersive VR technology can revolutionize physical therapy and remote rehabilitation. A big shoutout to the brilliant minds behind this innovation!
