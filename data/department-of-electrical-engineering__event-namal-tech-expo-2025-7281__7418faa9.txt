Home Event Namal Tech Expo 2025 Event Latest From Namal Namal Tech Expo 2025 The Department of Electrical Engineering at Namal University, Mianwali, proudly launched the Namal Tech Expo 2025 on April 14th with a dynamic opening ceremony, honored by the presence of our esteemed Rector, Prof. Dr. M. Najam Ul Islam. This landmark event brought together 451 talented students from 21 leading universities, forming 173 multidisciplinary teams from three provinces of Pakistan. The expo served as a vibrant hub of innovation, intellectual exchange, and creative problem-solving — showcasing the immense potential of the next generation of engineers and technologists. We were delighted to host distinguished guests, faculty, and participants whose enthusiasm and expertise set an inspiring tone for the days ahead. Namal Tech Expo 2025 is not just an event — it’s a celebration of innovation, collaboration, and academic excellence.
