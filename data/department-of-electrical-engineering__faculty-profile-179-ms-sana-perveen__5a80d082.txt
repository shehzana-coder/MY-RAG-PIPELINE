SANA PERVEEN Lab Engineer Department of Electrical Engineering 197 sana.perveen@namal.edu.pk Profile Publications Courses Projects Summary Sana Perveen has completed her Bachelor’s degree from UET Taxila and is a recipient of the Bronze Medal. Her Final Year Project focused on the classification and detection of lung nodules using advanced image processing techniques, aiming to support early diagnosis in medical imaging. Her research interests include Digital Signal Processing, Image Processing, Control Systems, and Industrial Automation. She has hands-on experience with Programmable Logic Controllers (PLCs) and their programming, which has strengthened her expertise in automation systems. She is proficient in Python and MATLAB and is dedicated to fostering student engagement through hands-on learning and the practical application of computational tools. Academic Background Bachelors of Science in Electrical Engineering (Detection and Classification of lung nodules using advanced image processing techniques, aiming to support early diagnosis in medical imaging.)
