Home Event Internal Audit of Electrical Engineering Department by External Evaluator Event Latest From Namal Internal Audit of Electrical Engineering Department by External Evaluator To ensure continuous improvement and academic excellence, the Quality Enhancement Cell (QEC) of Namal University, Mianwali successfully conducted the Internal Audit of the Electrical Engineering Department by the external reviewer on April 21, 2025. Dr. Momin Ayub Uppal, Head Electrical Engineering Department LUMS was invited as an external evaluator to review the BS EE Program of the Electrical Engineering Department. The aim of the audit was to evaluate quality standards, ensure compliance, and promote best practices, reaffirming our dedication to delivering exceptional education. We are grateful to our external evaluator and the Department of Electrical Engineering for their invaluable contributions!
