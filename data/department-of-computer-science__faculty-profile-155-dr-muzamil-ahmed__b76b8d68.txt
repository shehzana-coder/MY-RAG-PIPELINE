MUZAMIL AHMED Assistant Professor Department of Computer Science muzamil.ahmed@namal.edu.pk Profile Publications Courses Projects Summary Muzamil Ahmed holds a Ph.D. from COMSATS University Islamabad, Pakistan. He is a dedicated researcher with extensive experience in both academia and the IT industry, particularly in artificial intelligence and machine learning. His research has been published in esteemed peer-reviewed journals, including IEEE Transactions and ACM Transactions. Additionally, he is also an active reviewer for several prestigious journals, including Expert Systems (Elsevier), PLOS One, and IEEE Access. His expertise spans natural language processing, information retrieval, machine learning, and data science.
