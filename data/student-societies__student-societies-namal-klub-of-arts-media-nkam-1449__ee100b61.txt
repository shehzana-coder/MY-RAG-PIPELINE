Dramatic Club welcomes students who enjoy working on stage as well as behind the curtain, providing them a platform for artistic expression, dialogue and innovation in the arts of music and dramatics. Aims and Objectives: Give platform to those students who express themselves through art and music Bring out the artists in students. Promote art of music and acting in the students.
