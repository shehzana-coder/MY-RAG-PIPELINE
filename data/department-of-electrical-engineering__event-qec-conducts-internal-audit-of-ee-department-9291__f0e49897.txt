Home Event QEC Conducts Internal Audit of EE Department Event Latest From Namal QEC Conducts Internal Audit of EE Department To ensure continuous improvement and academic excellence, Quality Enhancement Cell (QEC) of Namal University, Mianwali successfully conducted the Internal Audit of the Electrical Engineering Department by the external auditors on March 20, 2025. Prof. Dr. Farhan Mahmood from UET Lahore and Prof. Dr. Ziaul Haq Abbas from Ghulam Ishaq Khan Institute (GIKI) were invited as external auditors to carry out the audit on PEC (OBE) standards. Aim of the audit was to assess quality standards, compliance, and best practices, reinforcing our commitment to delivering top-tier education. Grateful to our auditors and Department of Electrical Engineering for their valuable contributions!
