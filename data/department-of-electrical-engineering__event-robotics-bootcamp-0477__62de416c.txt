Home Event Robotics Bootcamp Event Latest From Namal Robotics Bootcamp The Department of Electrical Engineering at Namal University successfully organized a three-day hands-on Robotics Bootcamp. This event provided students with an opportunity to engage in practical learning experiences involving Line Following Robots, Zumo War, Sumo War, Balloon Bursting, and Lego Robots. The boot camp was designed to enhance students' technical competencies in robot design, programming, and real-world applications of autonomous systems. By integrating theoretical knowledge with hands-on implementation, participants developed a deeper understanding of robotics, automation, and artificial intelligence (AI). Such initiatives are crucial in equipping students with the necessary skills for cutting-edge advancements in robotics and AI, fostering innovation and research excellence in the field.
